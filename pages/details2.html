Holistic Traditions

Shambhala, in Tibetan Buddhist tradition, is a mythical kingdom often described as a spiritual paradise where all inhabitants have achieved enlightenment. Buddhist monks believe this is an actual place between the Himalayas and the Gobi desert where the earths energy and frequency is at its highest. Shambhala Holistics was born from an interest in Holistic healing, a belief in the healing power of touch and the positive effects of self care.
